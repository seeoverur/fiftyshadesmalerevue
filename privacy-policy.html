Privacy Policy

Learn how information submitted through this website may be collected, used, and protected.

Last Updated: April 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how information is collected, used, and protected when you visit or interact with this website and related services. This policy applies to information submitted through contact forms, direct inquiries, and general browsing activity on the site.

1. Information We Collect

When you use this website, we may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide, including your name, email address, phone number, event details, and any message content you choose to submit through the contact or booking form.

We may also collect non-personal information automatically through standard website technologies. This may include browser type, device type, pages visited, time spent on the site, referral source, and general usage patterns. This information helps us understand how visitors use the site and how the website can be improved.

2. How Your Information Is Used

Information collected through this website may be used to respond to inquiries, communicate about bookings, provide requested information, improve the user experience, and manage general website operations. Submitted information may also be used to follow up regarding services, availability, or event-related questions.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Contact Forms & Communication

When you submit a form through this website, the information you provide may be sent by email or stored securely for communication purposes. By submitting your information, you consent to being contacted regarding your inquiry using the details you provided.

4. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

This website may use cookies or similar technologies to improve user experience, analyze traffic, and better understand how visitors interact with the site. Cookies may help remember user preferences or assist with performance monitoring.

You may choose to disable cookies through your browser settings, though some site functionality may be limited as a result.

5. Third-Party Links

This website may contain links to third-party websites, social media pages, or partner services. These websites operate independently and may have their own privacy practices. We are not responsible for the content, policies, or security of third-party websites.

6. Data Security

Reasonable efforts are made to protect information submitted through this website from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure. However, no method of data transmission or online storage can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to request access to the personal information associated with you, request corrections, or request deletion of your information. Requests may be made using the contact methods provided on this website.

8. Children’s Privacy

This website is intended for adults only. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under 18 years of age.

9. Discretion Notice

Inquiries and communications submitted through this website are handled with discretion. Information shared for booking or contact purposes is used only to communicate about services and is not intentionally disclosed publicly.

10. Updates to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in services, website functionality, or legal requirements. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

11. Contact Information

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how information is handled, please use the contact section of the website to get in touch.

Questions About This Policy?
